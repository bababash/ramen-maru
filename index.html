SIGNATURE CHICKEN PAITAN
SIGNATURE CHICKEN PAITAN

Maru's Signature Dish: Thick, Creamy, Chicken Ramen.

Trending in Japan right now, the Paitan Ramen takes three days to prepare - every drop of this broth is packed with flavour. Further enhanced by Maru's dashi blend, chicken oil and speciality soy.

This ramen is served with thick cut poached chicken, a whole smoked egg, crispy parsnip, burnt garlic oil and handmade green noodles.

Paitan (鳥白湯) means white soup.

Maru's Dashi: A custom blend of local & Japanese dried fish with kombu, shiitake & sake.

Shoyu Tare: Made with koikuchi soy sauce (a less salty soy).

VEGETARIAN MISO
VEGETARIAN MISO

Red Miso, Oat Milk, Seasonal Vegetables.

Combining the complex flavour of red miso with creamy oat milk, hints of manuka honey & sesame, this ramen is packed with flavour. Maru's vegetarian dashi blend is an additional flavour bomb that takes this to the next level.

This ramen is served with fried eggplant, a whole smoked egg, red onion, pea sprouts, burnt garlic oil and special handmade green noodles.

Red Miso (赤みそ): Small grain fermented soybean paste.

CHICKEN CHINTAN SHIO

Yuzu Kawa: Japanese yuzu skin.

STORY OF MARU

Maru, an established Melbourne chef, felt the time had come for him to move to Japan's street food capital Osaka where he could follow his dream of understanding and appreciating the art of Japanese cuisine.

He realised that the only way to learn from the Japanese masters was to speak their language. He studied day and night until the doors of prestigious restaurants were opened for him.

Maru had arrived at the fore of Osaka's Ramen scene. New trends, flavour combinations, broths, dashi, and noodle techniques were being stirred up all around him. The masters had honed their craft and taught Maru their secret - do one thing and do it well.

Maru returned from his travels with an overflowing suitcase of traditional ingredients, a 300kg noodle machine in the cargo hold and a hope to share his ramen with you.
