Ramen Maru

Managing to escape during Melbourne's second lockdown, Maru moved to Osaka as an “English Teacher”, however teaching was not on the menu.

Learning enough Japanese to navigate through a job interview, Maru landed in the forefront of the Ramen scene. New trends, broth technique, noodle making and flavour combination were all on the radar during the limited time Maru had.

Observing the methods of hand made dashi from boss Yugo and the key to perfect noodle ‘mochi mochi’ texture from the expert and comedian Kenji.

These experiences, memories and flavours are all combined to create each proper bowl of Ramen Maru.
